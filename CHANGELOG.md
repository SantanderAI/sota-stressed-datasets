## [0.1.0] - 2026-06-17

### Added

- Initial public release of the **Stressed SOTA Datasets** collection.
- First dataset: **Stressed German Credit Dataset** (`SGCD-v0.1`), derived from
  the UCI Statlog (German Credit Data) dataset — 1,000 records with documented
  semantic shocks (`F0`–`F4`) and optional cosmetic noise.
- Collection-level `README.md`, `LICENSE` (CC BY 4.0), `NOTICE`, `CITATION.cff`,
  `CONTRIBUTING.md`, `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` and `SECURITY.md`.
